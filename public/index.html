Why I love Yoga

Yoga is a form of therapy to me. When you show up on the mat, you are giving yourself the gift of getting to know your body in an extremely intimate way.

Forms of Yoga

There are many forms of yoga and everyone will like something a different. Also, every teacher has a different style so people should try more than one teach for each style to see if they find someone or a form they like most.
